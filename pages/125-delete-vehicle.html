12.5. Delete Vehicle 

Purpose: To remove a vehicle on the system. The user will click the delete vehicle icon and confirm that they want to delete a vehicle, record of that vehicle will be removed from the system.

 

Nr

Item name

Item description

1

“Delete Vehicle” Icon.

Click on the ‘Delete Product category’ Icon. The system will display the “Delete product category” pop-up modal.

2

“Delete” button

click “Delete” button to confirm delete process.

3

“Cancel” button

click “Cancel” button to terminate delete process.

4

Success Message pop-up

Delete vehicle successful. Admin will click the “OK” button
