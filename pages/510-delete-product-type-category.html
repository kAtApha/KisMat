5.10. Delete Product Type Category

Purpose: To remove a product category type on the system. The user will click the delete product category icon and confirm that they want to delete a product type category, record of that product type category will be removed from the system.

 

 

 

Nr

Item name

Item description

1

‘Delete Product category’ Icon.

Click on the ‘Delete Product category’ Icon. The system will display the “Delete product category” pop-up modal.

2

“Delete” button

click “Delete” button to confirm delete process.

3

“Cancel” button

click “Cancel” button to terminate delete process.

4

Success Message pop-up

Delete product category successful. Admin will click the “OK” button

5

Unsuccessful Message pop-up

Delete product category unsuccessful as record is in use. Admin will click the “OK” button
