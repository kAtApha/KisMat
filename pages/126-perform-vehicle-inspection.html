12.6. Perform vehicle inspection

Purpose: To perform a new vehicle inspection on the system. The user will click the perform inspection icon and enter all the details of the new inspection, details will be saved on the system.

 

 

Nr

Item name

Item description

1

Inspector name textbox

Enters Inspector name

2

Inspector surname textbox

Enters Inspector Surname

3

“Drop-down” select.

Select inspection status from drop-down.

4

“Upload”

Upload inspection file.

5

“Add Inspection” button.

Click on the ‘Add Inspection’ button. The system will save inspection.

6

“Close” button.

Click on the “Close” button. The system will terminate process.

7

Success Message pop-up

Add ne inspection successful. Admin will click the “OK” button
